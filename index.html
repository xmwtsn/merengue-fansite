About Shortcake

Shortcake is a capsule fansite celebrating our favorite digital dessert rhinoceros: Merengue of Animal Crossing! This project was created for the September 2021 Code Louisville Front End Web Development Course by Xavier Watson.

Meet Merengue

Merengue is a rhino villager that appears in the Nintendo game series Animal Crossing. She is named for the sweet confection meringue, which is whipped egg whites and sugar. True to her name, Merengue resembles a strawberry shortcake, with strawberry and whipped cream horns, white points, a strawberry tail, and a pink body. Her default outfit is the Chef's Outfit.
