About Shortcake

Shortcake is a capsule fansite celebrating our favorite digital dessert rhinoceros: Merengue of Animal Crossing! This project was created for the September 2021 Code Louisville Front End Web Development Course by Xavier Watson.

Meet Merengue

Merengue is a rhino villager that appears in the Nintendo game series Animal Crossing. She is named for the sweet confection known as meringue, which is egg whites and sugar whipped to soft peaks. True to her name, Merengue resembles a strawberry shortcake, with a strawberry and whipped cream horn, a strawberry tail, creamy white points, and a pink body. Her default outfit is the Chef's Outfit.
